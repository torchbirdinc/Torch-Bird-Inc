Who We Are

Torch Bird Inc. is a dynamic food-service company headquartered in New Jersey, USA, operating as the holding and operational backbone for our fast-growing restaurant franchises and digital ventures.

We are proud franchisees of Hangry Joe's Hot Chicken, delivering Nashville-style heat and hospitality. Beyond the storefront, our team develops e-commerce, branded merchandise, and digital loyalty products, creating deeper connections with our community and B2B partners.
